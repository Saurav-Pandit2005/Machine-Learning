** Machine Learning
---------------------

-> Have you ever wondered how netflix knows what you want to watch next? or how Google Translate works like magic? The secret behind it all... is Machine Learning.

-> Instead of programming every step, we give Machines lots of data and let them figure out patterns on their own.

-> Today, Machine Learning powers everthing from the apps on your Phone to the system behind hospitals, banks, and even space research. Learning ML means you're Learning the langauge of the future.


** Real Life Machine Learning applications
---------------------------------------------

-> YouTube Recommendations -> Learns from your watch history.
-> Spam Detection in Gmail -> Learns pattern in spam emails.
-> Voice Assistants(Siri, Alexa) -> Learn how you speak.
-> Self-Driving Cars -> Learn to identify stop signs, pedestrians, and roads.
-> Face Unlock on Phones -> Learn to recogize your face.

** AI v/s ML v/s DL
----------------------

-> AI (Artificial Intelligence) is the broadest concept, ML (Machine Learning) is a subset of AI focused on learning from data, and DL (Deep Learning) is a specialized subset of ML that uses neural networks to handle complex tasks. 
-> In short: AI → ML → DL, each layer narrowing in scope but increasing in technical depth.


* Real-Life Analogies
------------------------

-> AI: The entire concept of making machines “think” (like a smart assistant).

-> ML: Teaching the assistant by showing examples (e.g., recognizing spam emails).

-> DL: Giving the assistant a “brain-like” structure (neural networks) so it can learn complex tasks like facial recognition or language translation.


* Key Takeaways
-------------------

-> AI is the umbrella → includes ML and DL.

-> ML is the engine → learns from data.

-> DL is the turbocharged engine → uses neural networks for advanced tasks.

-> Data & compute needs grow as you move from AI → ML → DL.
